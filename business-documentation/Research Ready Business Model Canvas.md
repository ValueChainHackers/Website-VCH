# Research Ready Business Model Canvas

## Key Partners
- Cloud infrastructure providers (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud)
- AI model developers and researchers
- Open-source communities
- Universities and research institutions
- Data providers
- System integrators

## Key Activities
- Development and maintenance of open-source AI tools and infrastructure
- Research and innovation in supply chain transparency
- Community building and support
- API development and management
- Documentation and training material creation

## Key Resources
- AI algorithms and models
- Software infrastructure and codebase
- Engineering and research team
- Computing resources (servers, cloud access)
- Data access and partnerships
- Intellectual property (open-source licenses)

## Value Propositions
- Accessible and affordable AI tools for supply chain transparency
- Open-source solutions fostering collaboration and innovation
- Scalable infrastructure for data analysis and visualization
- APIs enabling integration with existing systems
- Community support and knowledge sharing

## Customer Relationships
- Online forums and communities
- Documentation and tutorials
- API support
- Consulting services (for complex integrations)
- Enterprise support packages

## Channels
- Online platform and website
- GitHub and other open-source repositories
- Industry events and conferences
- Social media (LinkedIn, Twitter)
- Partnerships with system integrators

## Customer Segments
- Businesses seeking supply chain transparency solutions
- Researchers and academics
- NGOs and advocacy groups
- Government agencies
- System integrators

## Cost Structure
- Development and maintenance costs
- Cloud infrastructure costs
- Personnel costs (engineers, researchers)
- Marketing and community building expenses
- Data acquisition costs
- Legal and licensing fees

## Revenue Streams
- Consulting services
- Enterprise support packages
- API usage fees (tiered pricing)
- Sponsorships and grants
- Data licensing (aggregated and anonymized data)
