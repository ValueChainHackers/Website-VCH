**Tags/Keywords:** chocolate, cacao supply chain, transparency, ethics, traceability, Netherlands, research methods

**Project Summary:**
Beyond the Bar looks past the chocolate wrapper to investigate the complex supply chain behind it. This student research project developed frameworks for assessing chocolate supply chains, created business planning tools for ethical sourcing, and built technical solutions for tracking products from farm to shelf. The Dutch-language project reflects European priorities around supply chain transparency and fair trade.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Research framework for supply chain analysis
- Assessment tools for evaluating cacao sourcing ethics
- Business model canvas for transparent chocolate supply chains
- Python-based data extraction tools for research documents
- PDF solution guide for implementing traceability systems
- Documentation in Dutch for local industry partners
