The Dangerous Compliance Gap

EU regulations are pushing thousands of smallholder farmers out of markets

Farmers Can't Comply

  • EU laws (EUDR, CSDDD, CSRD) require proof of ethical sourcing
  • No tools to generate compliant documentation
  • Can't afford consultants or certification services

Importers Stuck Too

  • SME importers lack affordable due diligence tools
  • Risk: market exit or fraudulent compliance
  • Customs blocks shipments without proper docs

Result: Ethical sourcing becomes impossible at scale. Small farmers lose livelihoods. Supply chains consolidate to large players who can afford compliance.

How ClearRoots Works

1. Farmer Records Data

Mobile app (works offline) captures farm location, processing methods, harvest info

2. Blockchain Storage

Data syncs to AgUnity blockchain ledger creating tamper-proof records

3. AI Enhancement

Satellite imagery & GPS verify claims, fill missing data gaps automatically

4. Document Generation

System automatically generates EUDR, CSRD, CSDDD compliance docs. Importer downloads verified documentation for customs.
