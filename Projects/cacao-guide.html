Project Overview

The Cacao Guide is a comprehensive research resource exploring sustainable cacao supply chains and effective farmer support systems. This project examines best practices in ethical sourcing, fair trade principles, and sustainable agriculture in the cacao industry.

Developed through extensive research and field studies, the guide provides practical insights for businesses, researchers, and organizations working to improve sustainability in the chocolate industry.

Key Research Areas

Sustainable Farming

Best practices in sustainable cacao cultivation and environmental stewardship.

Farmer Support

Systems and programs to support smallholder cacao farmers and improve livelihoods.

Supply Chain Transparency

Traceability solutions and transparency initiatives in cacao sourcing.
