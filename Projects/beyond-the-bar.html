Project Overview

Beyond the Bar looks past the chocolate wrapper to investigate the complex supply chain behind it. This student research project developed frameworks for assessing chocolate supply chains, created business planning tools for ethical sourcing, and built technical solutions for tracking products from farm to shelf.

The Dutch-language project reflects European priorities around supply chain transparency and fair trade, providing practical tools for businesses looking to improve their sourcing practices.

Research Areas

Supply Chain Analysis

Research frameworks for evaluating cacao supply chain transparency and traceability.

Ethics Assessment

Tools for evaluating ethical sourcing practices and fair trade compliance.

Technical Solutions

Python-based tools for data extraction and document analysis.
