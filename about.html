About Value Chain Hackers

We're building bridges between academia and industry to solve the world's most pressing supply chain challenges.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 at Windesheim University, Value Chain Hackers emerged from a simple observation: the gap between academic research and real-world supply chain challenges was too wide.

We believe that the most innovative solutions come from collaboration. By bringing together students, researchers, and industry professionals, we create an environment where theoretical knowledge meets practical experience.

Today, we're a thriving community of over 200 members across 15 countries, working with leading companies to tackle everything from circular economy models to AI-driven logistics optimization.
